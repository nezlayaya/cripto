    This agreement (hereinafter referred to as the Agreement) describes the rules and conditions on the basis of which the services of the Kassa exchange service are provided and is an official written public offer addressed to individuals (hereinafter referred to as the User), conclude an Agreement on the provision of services by Kassa conditions set forth below. Use of Kassa service is possible only on condition that the User accepts all the terms of the Agreement.
    The current version of the Agreement is located for public access on the website of the Kassa service.

  • 2. Terms and definitions used in the Agreement

    Service Kassa (Service) - the system of providing Internet services for the exchange, sale and purchase of electronic currencies.
    The website of the Service is www.kassa.cc
    User is any individual or legal entity that uses the services of the Kassa Service and has accepted the acceptance of the Agreement in accordance with its terms.
    Partner - a person providing services to the Service to attract Users, the terms of which are described in this Agreement.
    Payment system- software product created by a third party, which is a mechanism for the implementation of accounting for cash (electronic money) and / or other obligations, payment for goods and services on the Internet, and the organization of mutual settlements between users.
    The main payment systems under this Agreement are:
    PerfectMoney, e-Voucher, Bitcoin, AdvCash, OkPay, Payeer, QIWI, Yandex.Money, WEX, Exmo and others. The exact list of payment systems is indicated on the website of the Service. The customer of the payment system is a person who has concluded an agreement with the corresponding payment system for the acquisition of property rights of the demand for it, measured in conventional units accepted in the corresponding payment system.
    E-currency- monetary and / or other obligation between the developer of this electronic asset and its user, expressed in a digital way.
    Payment / Transaction - transfer of electronic and / or other electronic assets from the payer to the recipient.
    The application is an expression of the User's intention to use one of the services offered by the Kassa Service by filling out the electronic form through the Service's website, on the terms described in the Agreement and specified in the parameters of this Application.
    Verification of the card is the verification of the belonging of the card (or account) to its owner. The conditions for checking membership are set by the Service, made at a time for each new customer account (card).
    Source Currency- an electronic currency that the User wishes to sell or exchange.
    Source account - the purse number or any other indication of the User's account in the Payment system from which the Source currency was sent.
    The received currency is the electronic currency that the User receives as a result of the sale or exchange of the Initial electronic asset.
